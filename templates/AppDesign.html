Basics of mobile navigation screen.

Research of mobile devices usage, found that 49% of people relay on a one thaumb to to get things don on their bi-phone.

Its important to place top level and frequently used actions at the bottom of the screen,because they are comfortably reached with one thumb interaction.

Design bottom navigation.

Many apps use the tab bar for the most important app's features.Facebook makes main pieces of core functinality available with one tap,allows rapid swithing justify-content-between features.

Make Navigation Intuitive

Learnability is key for perfect design success.Users should be able to navigate through your app via clear pathways and be able to complete all primary tasks without requiring any explanations.
User lack the patience to try to work through complex steps to get ehat they want. If it tackes too much time or effort to discover how to navigat through your product,chances are you're just going to lose your users.

Make Interface Elements Clearly Visible

People use their phones every where.Indoors,outdoors,in trains,planets,
buses,cars and underground etc. It's important to have sufficient contrast between content and background inyour design so it's legible, in any setting,even outside in the sunlight.But getting the right amount of contrast.

Provide User control

People like to feel like the are in controlnof their actions.So that's what you need to give them.
When the users to make decisions to personalize their journeys.Changing settings,controlling notification and cancelling actions providees the user with a sense of control over the system. App can suggest actions or provide warnings,but they should't be the ones doing the decision-making.
