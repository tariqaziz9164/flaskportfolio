Selectivety prioritize information.

This means that a good design must make decisions about what the user has to see and when. With specific design techniques such as size, color, and font style established priority are summed up in the principales of visual hierarchy.

Tell a story with images

Images are inevitable the most eye-catching part of a product design for a the simple reason that they are faster to scan than text. These days online shoppers expect to see an average of 6 images and at least 1 videos on product page. At the same time, images must be more than attention grabbing.This means that the images should creat an experience of the product, telling a story without words. We use images according to the product. For physical products,dimentions are often important,and this involves more than displaying the measurements. For products or services without a visual component,designing process should be more creative that envoke the experience of using the product.
